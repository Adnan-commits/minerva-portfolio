## A Note on How We Worked

Every member of the team contributed to identifying and proposing
new tools and capabilities throughout the project. The directions
Minerva grew in — from multi-format export to real-time streaming
to the Ask Minerva chat interface — came from ideas the whole
team brought to the table, not any single person.
